🚗SERVICES.🚗

 

CAR SERVICING & REPAIRING.

1)  Checking the operations of all  switches, lights, Horns, Washers, Wipers & Seat belts
2)  Lubrication of Door locks & Hinges
3)  Checking brake and clutch pedal, Free play & adjustment if  required.
4)  Checking parking brake lever stroke and play & adjustment if   required.
5)  Cleaning the Air purifier filter or replacement if required
6)  Checking V belts for cracks, fraying, wear & tear. Adjustment or replacement if necessary.
8)  Checking spark plugs, replacement if necessary.
9)  Checking engine coolant level, top up if required and check for leaks.
10)  Replacement of air cleaner element if required.
11)  Check battery electrolyte level, top up if required.
12)  Replacement of fuel filter if required (Only for vehicles where it is applicable).
13)  Check and refill washer fluid.
14)  Change brake fluid if necessary.
15)  Checking tires for abnormal wear and tear.
16)  Changing engine oil and filter, if required.
17)  Checking/top up manual transmission oil level.
18)  Checking/top up front & rear differential oil levels.
19)  Checking suspension system for damage and safety.
20)  Checking suspension arm ball joints for play & dust cover for  damage.
21)  Checking steering linkage for damage and loose connections.
22)  Checking & Top up power steering fluid.
23)  Lubrication of suspension arms, steering linkages & prop shafts.
24)  Checking brake hoses & pipes for leakage.
25)  Checking fuel hoses and pipes for leakage or deterioration.
26)  Checking exhaust systems for leaks & security.
27)  Checking all valves for proper functioning.
28)  Checking valve timing for diesel cars.
29)  Checking gearbox for play.
30)  Checking starter motor.
31)  Checking brake pads and brake liners for play.
32)  Checking clutch plate functioning and optimization.
33)  Checking A/C cooling and A/C filter.
34)  Checking for rust at critical areas hampering the performance of vehicle.
35)  Checking wheel alignment.
